The Alchemist (Trendy Bar)

This bar was the highlight of my night! I went with a few friends after a long day at work and was amazed at the range of cocktails on offer. Despite the steep prices (you won't find much different as we are in London), the vibe was very positive. The dimly lit atmosphere is perfect for chilling and some of the more expensive drinks change colour (mine even exploded!) I would definitely reccommend this venue- especially for a birthday as the bartenders are really chatty- I even got a free cocktail out of them!
